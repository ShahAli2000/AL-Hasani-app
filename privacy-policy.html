Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

AL-Hasani ("the App") is developed by Muntakhab Hasani. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Account and Authentication (Optional)

The App offers optional sign-in via Apple, Google, or email/password. Signing in enables:

  • Cloud sync of your settings, bookmarks, and reading progress across devices
  • Community features (creating events, mosque voting, group Quran reading, family prayer circles)

If you choose not to sign in, you can still use all core features (prayer times, Quran, duas, calendar, etc.) without any account. When you sign in, we store your email address and a unique user ID in Firebase Authentication (operated by Google).

2. Data We Collect

For all users (no account required):

  • A random device identifier (UUID) stored locally — not linked to your identity
  • App settings, bookmarks, reading progress — stored on your device only

For signed-in users:

  • Email address (from your Apple, Google, or email sign-in)
  • Settings, bookmarks, and reading progress synced to Firebase Firestore
  • Community contributions (events you create, mosque votes, group memberships)

3. Location Data

The App requests location permission solely to calculate prayer times, determine Qibla direction, and compute the local Hijri date. Your location is processed on your device. When you search for a location, your search query is sent to the Photon geocoding service (komoot.de). You can use the App without granting location permission by searching for a city manually.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your preferences and reading data locally using AsyncStorage, including: settings, bookmarks, reading progress, notes, prayer tracker counts, calendar events, and downloaded audio files. This data never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync.

5. Cloud Sync

Anonymous users: If you enable cloud sync without signing in, your data is stored in your personal iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android) account. We have no access to it.

Signed-in users: Your data is synced to Firebase Firestore (operated by Google). Sync is rate-limited to at most once every 5 minutes to minimise data transfers. You can sign out at any time to stop Firestore sync.

6. Community Features

When you use community features (creating events, voting on mosque denominations, group Quran reading, family prayer circles), the data you contribute is stored in Firebase Firestore and visible to other users. Event posters you upload are compressed and stored as part of the event data. Community contributions are associated with your user ID.

7. Image Processing (OCR)

When you upload an event poster and choose to read text from it, the image is sent to the Google Cloud Vision API for text extraction. Google may process this data according to their Cloud Privacy Notice. The image is not stored by us beyond the event posting.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Functions. Firebase Privacy.
  • Google Cloud Vision — Optional OCR for event posters. Privacy Notice.
  • Photon (komoot.de) — Location search autocomplete.
  • Overpass API (OpenStreetMap) — Mosque/centre data.
  • quran.com API — Word-by-word Quran data and audio.
  • alquran.cloud — Quran text and translations.
  • Apple/Google Maps — Map display, subject to their privacy policies.
  • iCloud / Google Drive — Only if you enable cloud sync without signing in.

9. Analytics

The App sends a single anonymous network request on launch to track general usage counts. This request contains no personal data, no device identifiers, and no location information.

10. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored until you uninstall the App or clear its data.
  • Firestore sync data: Stored while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Community contributions: Events are automatically cleaned up (poster images removed) 1 month after the event date.
  • Reports and moderation data: Retained for as long as needed for safety and legal compliance.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Use the in-app backup/export feature to download your settings and reading data.
  • Delete your account: Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account". Your account will be deactivated immediately. Data is retained for 30 days for recovery, then permanently deleted.
  • Withdraw consent: You can sign out, disable cloud sync, or revoke location permission at any time.
  • Data portability: Export your data via the in-app backup feature.

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at info@djali.co.uk.

12. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

13. Moderation

Community content may be reviewed by moderators. Users who violate community guidelines may have their content hidden and their account suspended. Moderation records are retained for safety purposes.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page with the updated date above.

15. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your data rights, contact us via the in-app feedback form or email info@djali.co.uk.
